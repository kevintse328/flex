About

Flex’s mission is to change the way people think about their careers and having a family. We believe talented people with caregiving responsibilities shouldn’t have to sacrifice a fulfilling career. We also know employers would benefit immensely from hiring such people.

To achieve our mission, Flex matches parents seeking flexible work with employers seeking professionals for part-time or project-based work. We want to help professionals maintain their skill sets and confidence as professionals while at home, and help employers hire highly qualified candidates while pushing forth their diversity agendas.
