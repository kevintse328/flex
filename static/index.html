How It Works

Apply to Flex

Tell us about your background and the type of work you’re looking for. We’ll get back to you in 3 business days if we have roles that match your skills, interests, and schedule. From there, you can apply to whichever roles you choose.

Get Personal Support

If you’re having trouble finding the right role, FLEX has a community of mentors just like you who have been through the freelancing process before. Ask us about getting assigned a mentor to provide career coaching, resume reviews, interview support, or whatever else you need.

Get Hired the Right Way

When you get the job, we’re available to help with contracting, incorporation, secure payment, project management, and even finding skills retraining resources. Our priority is finding you the perfect role, and you shouldn’t have to worry about anything but doing great work.

About

FLEX’s mission is to empower professionals to find fulfillment at work and at home by connecting them with flexible job opportunities. Our career management platform encourages professionals to engage with one another in a supportive community, and receive mentorship and guidance in their job undertakings.

Together, we’re building a new type of work style that fits into your life.
